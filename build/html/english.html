The process of displaying the registration page in the Personal Account and the Platform to the user¶

  1. Open R StocksTrader.

  1. Click GetStarted.

  1. Click the menu at the top left.

  1. Enter personal data, select a country.

  1. Check the boxes.

  1. If everything is entered correctly, a Personal Account will be opened.

  1. If the personal data is filled in with an error, then a corresponding message appears at the top.
